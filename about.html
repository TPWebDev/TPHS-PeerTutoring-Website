Our Mission

The TPHS Peer Tutoring Club is committed to fostering academic excellence and collaboration among Torrey Pines High School students. We believe in the power of peer-to-peer learning and strive to create a welcoming environment where students can receive personalized support in a variety of subjects.

What We Do

  • Host weekly tutoring sessions in the library.
  • Organize review workshops before major exams.
  • Offer one-on-one tutoring appointments by request.
  • Provide online resources and study guides.
